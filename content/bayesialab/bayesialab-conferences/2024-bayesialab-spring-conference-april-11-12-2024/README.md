# 🇺🇸 2024 BayesiaLab Spring Conference — April 11–12, 2024

### Welcome to the 2024 BayesiaLab Spring Conference

**Graduate Hotel Cincinnati, 151 Goodman Drive,  Cincinnati, Ohio 45219**\
**April 11–12, 2024**

The biannual BayesiaLab conference stands out as the premier event dedicated to applied research with Bayesian networks. Our conference aims to promote the practical deployment of Bayesian networks in research, analytics, and problem-solving.

What started in 2013 in Orlando, Florida, as a modest gathering has now blossomed into an international event, drawing attendees from various corners of the world. Over the years, it has fostered a vibrant community comprising researchers and professionals from many domains.&#x20;

### FAQ

<details>

<summary>What is the cost for attending this conference?</summary>

General admission for the two-day conference is $399 (plus Eventbrite fees and taxes).&#x20;

Participants from academic institutions, NGOs, government agencies, and the military are eligible for a discounted conference ticket of $299 (plus Eventbrite fees and taxes).

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is the typical background of conference attendees?</summary>

Applied researchers, statisticians, data scientists, data miners, decision scientists, biologists, ecologists, environmental scientists, epidemiologists, predictive modelers, econometricians, economists, market researchers, knowledge managers, marketing scientists, operations researchers, social scientists, students and teachers in related fields.

</details>

<details>

<summary>I'm new to Bayesian networks. Does it make sense for me to attend?</summary>

By all means, yes! Most attendees consider themselves students rather than experts in the rapidly evolving field of Bayesian network research, so even if you are entirely new to this field, you'll fit right in with everybody else on this journey of discovery.

</details>

<details>

<summary>Can I attend this conference remotely?</summary>

No, this is an in-person event, and you will need to be present at the venue in Cincinnati.

</details>

<details>

<summary>What is the cancellation and refund policy?</summary>

You may cancel your registration for a full refund of the fee up to 30 days before the start of the event. If you cancel within 30 days of the event, your registration fee will not be refunded. However, you will be able to apply 100% of the paid fees towards future BayesiaLab events.

</details>
